An epic journey of a circle in search of lost treasures and love.

Controls:

arrow keys or WASD - Move circle.
Spacebar or Enter - Interact with the golden flower.  Boosts for a short time. Repel evil squares.
Esc - Brings up the menu. Exit puts you back at the beginning.

After the first level, everything you touch or touches you is harmful.  You'll know this when the screen starts blurring.  So watch out!
